      What are Clinical Pathways?

      Clinical pathways guide Florence's AI decisions during phone conversations. Each pathway includes red flags, triage questions, self‑care instructions, and clear escalation triggers. Everything happens through natural conversation — Florence works under your protocols, not as a replacement for medical care. Your patients get consistent, protocol‑driven triage at 2am Sunday just as they would during office hours.
