NightingaleMD Showcases First End‑to‑End CMS "Kill the Clipboard" Solution With Provider‑Controlled AI Voice Navigator

Washington, DC — July 31, 2025

Yesterday CMS and the White House introduced the Health‑Tech Ecosystem. NightingaleMD’s Florence™ AI Voice Navigator was the only solution live on stage, recognized by keynote speaker Dr. Mehmet Oz and CMS innovation lead Amy Gleason.

True read‑write FHIR in < 10 seconds

Florence imports a patient’s consolidated record from CMS‑aligned networks, generates a SMART Health Card face‑sheet for the clinic, then writes the visit back in the same FHIR R4 bundle—completely clipboard‑free.

Engineered by physicians — for their patients

Florence’s conversational AI operates under primary‑care physician delegation and escalates to the practice’s nurse or MD whenever protocols dictate. Design decisions come from practicing clinicians, not pure technologists.

30‑language chronic‑care coaching

Florence already guides COPD, diabetes and obesity care in 30 languages, using real‑time vitals, meds and labs.
